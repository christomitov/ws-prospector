## Milestone 1 (current)

Scope in progress:

- In-page right drawer injection on LinkedIn profiles (with top-right collapsible launcher)
- Local profile parsing (+ section context for About/Experience/Activity)
- Local storage of prospect + activity
- Provider-routed AI enrichment (OpenAI/Gemini) + AI-provided classification and signals
- Single-prompt (modal-edited) AI draft generation and copy action
- Settings editor for prompts, rules, defaults, AI provider/model/keys
- Debug logging with CLI tail support
